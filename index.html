LEARN ABOUT GAME

INTRODUCTION : This Game is about rolling a die and trying to get as many point as possible without rolling 1, the goal of the game is to get 100 point. The first player to get to 100 is pronuced as the Winner

  • You can play with a Friend
  • You can play with the AI i created special for you

    • HOW TO PLAY

  • To roll the die click on the roll button
  • To save the record click on the save button
  • To refresh the Game click on the refresh image rolling by the bottom right
  • To off sound click on the sound blow this information
  • To bring this information back click on the about above the refresh image rolling by the bottom right

    • RULES

  • This is an example : If active player rolls 2 the 2 will stored in the record automatically, if active player rolls again and got 4 the 4 will be added to the 2 in the record which makes record = 6, if player clicks on save the current number in the record will be stored in the active player's score space and the active player will automatically switch to the next player.
  • If active player decides not to save and clicks on roll and active player got 1 from roll, the current value in players record which was 6 will be lost and active player will automatically switch to the next player
  • The first to get to 100 will be decleared as the WINNER

    • Advise

  • It is advised to play wisely and not to be greedy
